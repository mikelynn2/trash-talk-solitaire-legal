Trash Talk Solitaire

Classic Klondike. Unsolicited opinions.

How to play

Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace through King.

  • Draw: Tap the stock pile at top left. Tap its ×1/×3 badge to switch Draw mode.
  • Move: Tap a face-up card and then its destination, or drag it directly.
  • Build the tableau: Stack downward in alternating red and black suits.
  • Use empty columns: Only a King, with any cards attached to it, may fill an empty column.
  • Send cards home: Double-tap a playable card to move it to a foundation.
  • Get help: Tap the lightbulb for a useful move. Tap the back arrow to undo.

Modes and scoring

Draw 1 and Draw 3

Draw 1 exposes one stock card at a time. Draw 3 exposes cards in groups of three and is usually more challenging.

Deal difficulty

Easy favors early access to lower cards. Medium keeps the classic feel while periodically choosing a solver-verified friendly deal. Hard creates a more punishing deal. A new difficulty applies to the next deal.

Vegas scoring

Vegas mode uses fictional dollars only. A hand begins at −$52 and earns $5 for each card moved to a foundation. Starting a new hand before finishing records the current hand as abandoned. There is no real money, purchase, prize, or cash-out.

Voice, sounds, and haptics

The butler's visible commentary works offline and remains on screen when speech is muted.

  • Spoken Commentary: Turns the on-device British voice on or off.
  • Speech Rate: Changes how quickly the butler speaks.
  • Test Voice: Plays a short sample using the current rate.
  • Sound Effects: Controls card and celebration sounds separately.
  • Haptics: Controls tactile card, hint, warning, and achievement feedback.

Frequently asked questions

Does the game need an account or internet connection?

No. Gameplay, commentary, speech, statistics, and achievements work without an account or app-initiated network connection.

Does the game collect my data?

No. Preferences, aggregate statistics, and achievements stay in the app's local storage. Read the dedicated privacy policy.

Will my current hand return after I terminate the app?

Yes. The active deal and its undo history are saved locally so the hand can resume on the same device.

How do I reset my statistics?

Open Settings, scroll to Statistics, and choose Reset Stats. This also clears local achievements.
