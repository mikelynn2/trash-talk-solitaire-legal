Trash Talk Solitaire

Privacy policy

Last updated July 12, 2026

Short version: Trash Talk Solitaire is an offline-first card game operated by 405 Capital LLC. The app does not require an account and does not automatically send us personal information or gameplay data.

Who operates the app

405 Capital LLC (“405 Capital,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates Trash Talk Solitaire and is responsible for the practices described in this policy.

Data collection by the app

The app does not automatically collect or transmit personal data or gameplay data to 405 Capital LLC.

  • No account, login, or contact information
  • No location access
  • No analytics, advertising, attribution, or tracking services
  • No advertisements
  • No game server or background network requests
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Information stored on the device

The app stores game preferences, audio preferences, aggregate statistics, streaks, fictional Vegas bankroll, achievements, and the current in-progress deal with its local undo history in its own app container. This information is used only to provide and resume the game on that device. The developer does not receive it.

The operating system may include app-container data in a device backup if the device owner enables backup services. Those backups are controlled by the device owner and platform provider, not Trash Talk Solitaire.

The active-deal file is replaced as play changes and cleared when it is no longer needed. Removing the app also removes its local data, subject to any device backups controlled by the device owner and platform provider.

Spoken commentary

Spoken commentary is generated from the same on-screen text using Apple's on-device speech synthesizer. Trash Talk Solitaire does not record microphone audio, upload speech, or send commentary text to a developer-operated service. Optional system voices are installed and managed by the operating system.

Links and optional sharing

Gameplay works without a game server. The app accesses an external service only when you choose to open a privacy or support link, or choose to share a game result through Apple's system share sheet. The recipient or service you select processes anything you choose to share under its own privacy terms. 405 Capital LLC does not receive the shared content through the app.

This website

This policy is hosted by GitHub Pages. When you visit it, GitHub may process ordinary web-request and security information—such as your IP address, browser or device information, requested URL, and request timing—to deliver and protect the page. GitHub handles that information under its privacy statement. 405 Capital LLC does not use advertising cookies or app-owned web analytics on this page.

Support communications

If you voluntarily contact 405 Capital LLC for support, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message, device details, and attachments. We use it only to respond, troubleshoot, protect the service, and meet legal obligations. Do not send passwords or sensitive personal information.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information or share it for targeted advertising. Information voluntarily provided for support may be processed by service providers that help us operate email or support services, or disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, protect rights and safety, or prevent abuse. Those providers are permitted to process information only for the services they provide to us.

Retention, deletion, and choices

On-device game information remains on your device until it is replaced, reset through available app controls, or removed by uninstalling the app, subject to device backups you control through the platform provider. Support communications are kept only as long as reasonably needed to resolve the request, maintain appropriate business records, protect the service, and meet legal obligations.

Because the app does not create an account or send us its local game data, 405 Capital LLC has no server-side game profile to access or delete. You can stop optional sharing by not using the share sheet, and you can stop website access by not opening external links. To ask about a support communication or request its deletion, email hello@tinroof.ai.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal information through a support communication without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete it. The App Store age rating is determined separately from Apple's current content questionnaire, including the app's optional fictional Vegas scoring.

Policy changes

If the app's data practices change, this page and the App Store privacy answers will be updated before the changed version is released.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact 405 Capital LLC at hello@tinroof.ai.
